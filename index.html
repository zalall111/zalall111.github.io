    Welcome to Zhou Xie's Homepage!!

    I am a Sophomore majoring in Data Science and Big Data Technology at Yangtze University and have been fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Guozhong Gao. For more information, please check my CV.

    My current research interests are in Machine Learning and Computer Vision. In particular, I am interested in the applied aspects of Image Information Processing and Knowledge Distillation, and aim to further investigate intelligent image processing using knowledge distillation techniques. Prior to this, I explored the detection of point cloud data, with a focus on 3D image data processing.

    If you are interested in my research, please feel free to email me.
